About me

Shubham Gour is an employee working in an IT firm and currently pursuing a Master's degree while balancing a full-time job. Shubham draws inspiration for his writing from personal experiences and observations, as well as the unwavering support of his parents.

As someone who has faced judgment and criticism, Shubham believes that it is important to rise above negativity and overcome obstacles in order to achieve success. He has put this belief into practice by writing two books, NO PAIN and RISEN UP, during the pandemic, using his experiences to motivate and inspire others.

More about me

With his background in IT and his passion for self-improvement, Shubham is uniquely qualified to write on the topic of personal development. Through his books, he hopes to encourage readers to pursue their dreams and overcome their own challenges, no matter how difficult they may seem.

Above all, Shubham's message to readers is clear: don't let others hold you back from achieving your dreams. With hard work, determination, and a positive attitude, anything is possible.
